Use Power+ on Canvas

Step 1: Install Power+

If you don't already have Power+ installed, click here to install it


Step 2: Opt-in to Power+ for Canvas testing

Login to your Canvas account, then click on your profile picture on the sidebar at the left and select Settings. On the settings page, scroll down to Power+ (canary) under the list of feature options and click the slider to enable it.


Step 3: Use Power+

Click the dashboard button in the sidebar, then click Power+ in the sidebar to start using Power+ on Canvas
