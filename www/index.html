Power+

Manage your coursework

Power+ shows you upcoming assignments, a calendar, and updates at a glance

Grade Calculation

Power+ calculates your CBL class grades and lets you test what-if grades

Privacy

Privacy summary

  • Power+ does not collect any personal information. All personal information shown in Power+ is fetched directly from Canvas to your device. All processing of personal information (such as CBL grade calculation) is done on-device.
  • Prefrences and grade history are stored on-device and are not associated with your Canvas account.
  • Power+'s code is fully open-source on GitHub.

Data collection

  • Power+ collects basic non-personally identifiable information to understand how people use Power+. This helps determine how Power+ can be improved to meet its user's needs and to make sure Power+ works as intended across a variety of users and use cases.
  • Power+ uses Google Analytics to collect data. Data collected does not use Google account signals, is not used for ad personalization, and is not shared with Google or other third parties.
  • Power+ collects the information listed below:
    • Basic device/session information (including, but not limited to):
      • Operating system
      • Browser
      • IP Address
      • Session data (including how often and how long you use Power+)
      • How you access Power+ (e.g. Canvas sidebar, bookmarklet, URL, etc.)
      • The URL and LMS that you use Power+ with
    • Basic user information:
      • User ID hash (the user ID collected by Power+ is a hash of your LTI ID and cannot be traced back to you)
      • User role (e.g. student, parent, etc.)
      • Usage data (including what features you use)
      • The number of classes you use Power+ with
  • To opt-out of analytics, go to Settings -> About -> Advanced Options -> Analytics opt-out or install the Google Analytics opt-out extension. The opt-out only applies to the browser and may be reset if cookies are cleared.
  • If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy on Power+ please contact me at privacy@jottocraft.com.
